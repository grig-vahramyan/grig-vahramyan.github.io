Based in Yerevan, Armenia, I am a passionate film director dedicated to turning visions into powerful visual narratives. With years of experience in the industry, I specialize in music videos, short films, and commercial projects that resonate with audiences on an emotional level.

My approach blends cinematic technique with raw artistic expression, ensuring each project has its own unique voice and identity. From concept to final cut, I guide every frame towards a singular creative vision.
